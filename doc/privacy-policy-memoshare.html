Privacy Policy — MemoShare

Last updated: July 13, 2026

MemoShare (package com.cekli.memoshare) is a photo editor and local sharing app that stamps, edits, and organizes photos — including those captured by MemoCam.

MemoShare has no login system, no user account, and no analytics or advertising SDK. It does not sell or share personal data with third parties. All data described below stays on your device unless explicitly noted.

Data collected

DataWhy it's collectedWhere it goes
Photos/media library accessCore app function — browsing, editing, and stamping existing photosStays on your device
Face detection (on-device ML Kit)Powers the beautify/editing tool's skin and face-aware effectsProcessed entirely on-device using Google's ML Kit; no image or face data is uploaded anywhere
Wi-Fi state / nearby Wi-Fi devicesLocal network photo sharing (ShareZone) between your own devicesTransferred only over your local network; never sent to the internet or to the developer

Data not collected

  • No camera, microphone, or location access — MemoShare does not capture photos/video or read GPS
  • No account registration or sign-in
  • No analytics or crash-reporting SDKs
  • No advertising SDKs — the app shows no ads
  • No data is sold, rented, or shared with third parties for advertising or any other purpose
  • No data is transmitted to the developer's own servers — the app has no backend

Third-party services

MemoShare uses Google's ML Kit Face Detection library entirely on-device to power editing features. No photo, face, or biometric data is uploaded to Google or any other server as part of this processing.

Data retention & deletion

All photos, edits, and settings are stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes its local settings. Photos saved to your device's gallery (MediaStore) persist independently of the app and can be deleted at any time using your device's Photos/Gallery app.

Children's privacy

MemoShare is not directed at children, does not knowingly collect data from children, and contains no ads or in-app purchases.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as the app evolves. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date above.
