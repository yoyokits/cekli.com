Privacy Policy — MemoCam

Last updated: July 13, 2026

MemoCam (package com.cekli.memocam) is a camera app that captures photos and video with live sensor overlays (location, compass, weather).

MemoCam has no login system, no user account, and no analytics or advertising SDK. It does not sell or share personal data with third parties. All data described below stays on your device unless explicitly noted.

Data collected

DataWhy it's collectedWhere it goes
Camera (photos & video)Core app function — capturing photos/videosStored only on your device (local storage / MediaStore)
Microphone audioRecording audio alongside video captureStored only on your device, embedded in the video file
Precise & approximate location (GPS)Embedding location metadata (EXIF GPS tags) into captured photos/videos, and showing a live location overlay on the viewfinderStored only in the photo/video file on your device
Device sensors (compass/orientation)Showing a live compass heading overlay and writing direction into photo EXIF metadataStored only on your device
Approximate coordinates (for weather lookup)Fetching current weather/temperature to display on the overlaySent to the third-party Open-Meteo weather API as anonymous coordinates only — no account identifiers, device IDs, or other personal data are sent
Photos/media library accessDisplaying your camera roll in the in-app gallery, viewing/editing previously captured photosStays on your device
Wi-Fi state / nearby Wi-Fi devicesLocal network photo sharing (ShareZone) — lets another device on the same Wi-Fi network browse/download your photos directly from your phoneTransferred only over your local network to a device you choose; never sent to the internet or to the developer

Data not collected

  • No account registration or sign-in
  • No analytics or crash-reporting SDKs
  • No advertising SDKs — the app shows no ads
  • No data is sold, rented, or shared with third parties for advertising or any other purpose
  • No data is transmitted to the developer's own servers — the app has no backend

Third-party services

MemoCam sends only anonymous latitude/longitude coordinates to Open-Meteo to retrieve current weather conditions for the overlay. No other personal data accompanies this request, and Open-Meteo does not receive any identifying information about you or your device.

Data retention & deletion

All photos, videos, and settings are stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes its local settings. Photos saved to your device's gallery (MediaStore) persist independently of the app, as with any camera app, and can be deleted at any time using your device's Photos/Gallery app.

Children's privacy

MemoCam is not directed at children, does not knowingly collect data from children, and contains no ads or in-app purchases.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as the app evolves. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date above.
